# ClearView

ClearView makes blocks between you and the third person camera fade away.

So instead of the camera getting pushed into your face every time a wall, tree, roof, cave bit, or whatever else gets in the way, the view stays readable.

Client side only.

## Why

I had this idea while playing The Sims 2 Castaway on ps2, entering my little hut!

So I made this as a challenge to myself: getting that kind of effect working in Vintage Story without using shaders.
